About Our School

A Legacy of Learning & Excellence

Orchid English Secondary School has been a trusted name in quality education in Harion, Sarlahi. We blend strong Nepali values with modern, English-medium pedagogy, creating an environment where every student can thrive academically, artistically and athletically.

Affiliated with the National Examinations Board (NEB), we offer a complete educational journey from Pre-School through Grade 12 (+2) in Science and Management streams.
