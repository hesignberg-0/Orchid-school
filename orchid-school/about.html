About Orchid

A trusted English-medium school in Harion, Sarlahi, nurturing curious minds and shaping confident, compassionate leaders since our founding.

Who We Are

Education That Goes Beyond the Classroom

Orchid English Secondary School is dedicated to delivering quality, English-medium education that balances academic rigour with character, creativity and care.

Located in the heart of Harion, Sarlahi, our campus offers a complete educational journey, from Pre-School (PG–UKG) through Grade 10, and on to +2 in Science and Management streams. Affiliated with the National Examinations Board (NEB), we combine a strong, structured curriculum with modern teaching methods and a warm, supportive culture.

Every child at Orchid is known, valued and encouraged to discover their own purpose.
